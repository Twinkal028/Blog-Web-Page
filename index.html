OVER STORY

In the vibrant city of Raipur, the aroma of freshly baked goods mingled with the bustling energy of its streets. It was the year 2022 when a little bakery named "Twink's Bakery" opened its doors, setting the stage for a sweet and remarkable journey. The bakery was the brainchild of Riya, a passionate baker who had always dreamed of sharing her love for baking with the world. She was lovingly nicknamed "Twink" by her friends, owing to her infectious enthusiasm and twinkling eyes whenever she talked about her creations. Twink's Bakery was a haven of delectable treats and cozy charm. With its pastel-colored walls adorned with vintage baking utensils and the inviting scent of freshly baked bread wafting through the air, it quickly became a favorite spot for locals and visitors alike.
